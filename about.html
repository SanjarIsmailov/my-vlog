ABOUT ME

From 2023 to 2024, I undertook an intensive programming course at PDP Academy, focusing on Java backend development. This transformative experience provided me with a robust understanding of backend technologies, equipping me with skills crucial for building scalable and efficient applications.

At PDP Academy, I delved deep into core Java concepts, Spring Boot framework, database management, and RESTful API development. The curriculum emphasized both theoretical knowledge and practical application, allowing me to work on real-world projects and understand the nuances of backend architecture.

Collaborating with peers and mentors enriched my problem-solving abilities and fostered a deeper appreciation for clean, maintainable code. Through this journey, I also gained expertise in tools like Git, Maven, and PostgreSQL, further solidifying my technical foundation.

This period not only enhanced my technical skills but also shaped my ability to adapt, learn, and grow in the dynamic field of software development.
